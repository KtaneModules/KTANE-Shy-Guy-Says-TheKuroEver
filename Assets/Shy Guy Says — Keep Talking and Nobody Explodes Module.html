On the Subject of Shy Guy Says

Hey, you're not Simon!

To activate the module, press the Shy Guy in the middle. When activated, a 40 second timer will begin, and the flags will flash a sequence of 7 flashes, each one a random color (Red, White, Green, or Blue) and with a random letter (A, B, L, or R) on them.

Use the flag's colors and letters to determine which of the flags to press. Then, press those flags in order to clear the stage before the timer runs out. Press the Shy Guy again after clearing the current stage to begin the next stage. Each sequential stage's flashes will flash quicker than the previous stage. Clear all 3 stages to disarm the module.

If the timer runs out, an incorrect input is made, or the Shy Guy is pressed during a stage, the module will strike and reset to the beginning of the current stage. The Shy Guy will need to be pressed to receive the sequence again.
